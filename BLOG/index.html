Tech For The Future

A look at New Tech set to Showcase @CES2026

IphoneZ

Apple's newest iPhone will utilize the companies latest A17 chip. Though it has not yet been released this chip is said to 10x faster than its predecessor and about 25x faster than the older iPhone 12. The iPhoneZ will also come with IOS16 pre-install, which introduces "FACETIME AR", an augmented reality experience that redefines staying digitally connected.

AppleWatch Series 8

Apple

The Series8 will utilize the same technology from the newest line of iPhones to introduce smart projections. Now notifications and other information from 1st party apps can be projected into real-world settings for a bigger virtual display.

Macbook Flex

Apple

The MacBookFlex will be Apple's first attempt at a 2-in-1 laptop. This machine will come installed with the new M3 chip and sport a 3d 8k display with touchscreen capabilities.

Features

  • 4,000,000 to 1 Cntrast ratio
  • 32 mp camera
  • FaceTime AR
  • 3D Screen
  • New TouchID
  • FaceID Support
  • 7 days of Screen-On Time

Aston Martin: Gorrion SeriesW

The Gorrion will be the first of Aston Martin's luxury aerial line. Comfortably seating 5 people, this car and personal plane hybrid uses a quad-wing system the lets drivers soar through the air at speeds similar to on land. The front wings also retract to allow the model to morph into a street-legal sports car. Though it will be showcased, there are no plans to release the car until at least 2030.

Features

  • On-Off Road Driving/ In-Air Flying
  • 8k capability
  • USB E + HDMI CONNECTIVITY
  • 120FPS Gaming

Switch +

Nintendo

The Nintendo Switch + is the successor to 2022 's Switch Pro. Ripping a page out of the book of its DS ancestor, the new device sports a new dual-screen design. The Switch + will also support 8k gaming, similar to its competion.

Features

  • Foldable Dual screens
  • 8k capability
  • USB E + HDMI CONNECTIVITY
  • 120FPS Gaming

Playstation 5 Pro

Playstation

The Playstation 5 Pro gives the already successful PS5 an internal overhaul, to allow for an amazing gaming experience. The new eternal design works with the redesigned cooling system, making for the quietest console in Sony's line.

Xbox Series XL

Microsoft

Similar to its rival, the Xbox series XL give its predecessor an internal overhaul, to allow for an amazing gaming experience. The system also comes with new backwards compatibility abilities.
